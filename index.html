This interactive essay was created using data from the Digital Yoknapatawpha database. It supplements the long-form essay available in the Norton Critical Edition of Absalom, Absalom!. It includes visualizations that are not possible to represent in print, and also functions as a standalone essay that explores Absalom, Absalom! along three different axes: Characters, Events, and Language.
